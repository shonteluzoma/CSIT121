Evaluation Questions

What is the URL of the website?
https://www.webtoon.com
What is the name of the website?
Webtoon
Who is the site's target audience?
It's mainly for comic fans, especially younger readers like high school and college students who love webcomics and digital stories.
How is the site organized?
Webtoon.com is set up with a home page, different genres, and sections for featured comics and recommendations. The navigation is pretty straightforward so you can easily find new comics or your favorites.
Which CRAP Design Principle does the site use? Provide at least one example.
The site uses a high contrast between the text and background, making it easier to read. For example, the dark text on a light background really helps with readability.
What is the Audit Score according to the Accessibility Checker?
The Accessibility Checker gave it a score of 92/100. Not perfect, but pretty good overall with a few areas that need a little work.
What is the site's effectiveness? Does it support users in completing actions accurately?
The site is effective because it lets users easily navigate through comics, create profiles, and interact with content without much confusion.
What is the site's efficiency? Can users perform tasks quickly?
The layout is clean and intuitive, so users can quickly find what they're looking for and perform tasks like reading or rating comics without delays.
How is the engagement? Is it pleasant to use and appropriate for its industry/topic?
It has cool visuals and interactive elements that keep users interested, and it fits the vibe of digital comics really well.
Recommendation to Improve the Website
One suggestion is to improve keyboard navigation. Making sure all interactive elements show a clear focus state would help users who rely on keyboards.
